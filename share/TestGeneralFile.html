Evony General: Himiko

    • Charlie Greener
    • November 6, 2023

    “The queen of Yamataikoku in Japan, gifted with Kiido magic.”

    Himiko provides the following buffs:

    • Enemy Mounted Troop Attack -25%
    • Ranged Troop Attack and Defense +15

    Himiko – Stats:

    Base Stats:

    Leadership: 112

    Attack: 113

    Defense: 106

    Politics: 111

    Basic Information:

    Availability: Tavern

    Cost: 40,000,000 Gold

    Culture: Japan

    Ascendable: Yes

    Special Skill:

    Kiido Magic

    Reduces enemy mounted troops’ attack by 25% and increases your ranged troops’ attack and defense by 15% when General is leading the army.

    Ascended Special Skill Buffs

    Below are the additional buffs that an ascended Himiko will receive to his Special Skill for each ascended star level.

    • 1 Star – Ranged Troop Attack +5% and Defense +15%.
    • 2 Star – Increases Troop Death into Wounded Rate by 8% when General is leading the army to attack.
    • 3 Star – When General is launching Alliance War, Rally Capacity +6%.
    • 4 Star – Ranged Troop Attack +10% and HP +15%.
    • 5 Star – Ranged Troop Attack +10%, Defense and HP +10%.

    Himiko – Specialities:

    General Specialties are unlocked at General level 25. Specialities consume runestones and gems – for more information check out our guide here.

    Ranged Troop Assault

    • Ranged Troop Attack +10%
    • Ranged Troop HP +10%

    Snipe

    • Enemy Troop Attack -10%

    Iron Warrior

    • Enemy Wounded into Death when attacking +10%

    Queen of Yamataikoku

    • Enemy Ranged Troop Attack -26%
    • Enemy Mounted Troop Attack -26%
